Sight and Life is on a quest to end malnutrition.Given the mounting evidence that eggs can improve maternal and child nutrition in developing countries, we are implementing projects and new innovations in egg production, egg-based products, models, tools and approaches to address protein and micronutrient malnutrition during pregnancy, infancy, and adolescence.

To support countries and partners working in this area, we have developed the egg hub website - a one-stop shop for information on egg production and consumption in LMICs, including policy publications, research studies and evaluations and success stories from around the world.

Egg Hub provides an oppurtunity to everyone to have a clear analysis of the egg supply and demand across the globe. This makes it easier for the stakeholders, poultry industry, investors to do their jobs right.
