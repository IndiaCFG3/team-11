egghub


Various supply and demand side challenges persist in making eggs accessible to the most vulnerable. At Sight and Life, we are working to address these barriers and ensure that eggs are available and affordable to low-income households. Through our Eggciting Program, we are supporting smallholder poultry business models in Kenya, Ethiopia, Malawi and India. We are also piloting new innovations in egg production, egg-based products, models, tools and approaches to address protein and micronutrient malnutrition among the most vulnerable groups, women, and children.


EGGHUB.ORG, is a one-stop resource for information, and research related to egg consumption and production in low and middle-income countries (LMICs), aiming to improve collaboration and innovation around eggs, two elements that are key to increasing the availability of eggs globally.
